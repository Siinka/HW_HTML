Meet Aiko the Cat

Your friendly feline with superpowers!

Biography

Aiko is a Scottish straight cat with a blue-point coat and striking blue eyes. At first glance, he might seem a little grumpy (he does love to voice his displeasure from time to time), but deep down, he's the sweetest and kindest cat. He loves his mom and dad with all his heart, though he prefers to keep his affection subtle. His favorite pastime? Having midnight conversations with the moon and watching the world from the window as if he's a true night guardian.

Hobbies

When the sun sets, Aiko comes alive! He’s a master of the midnight "zoomies," complete with loud songs and high-speed sprints around the house. \But don't be fooled by his serious daytime demeanor—Aiko is a true explorer at heart. He loves climbing into the most unexpected spots, squeezing into any box or bag he can find. His fur has a special way of leaving "souvenirs" on every piece of black clothing in the house. And of course, he’s got a soft spot for canned chicken and beef, but here's the kicker: he has a peculiar fondness for beer!

Favorite Food

Aiko is a discerning eater—only canned chicken and beef for this regal cat. He also has a secret: an odd but undeniable love for beer. It’s rare, but when he gets a sniff of that hoppy aroma, his curiosity piques!

Favorite People

Aiko’s favorite humans are, of course, his mom and dad, and anyone kind enough to open his canned food. He shows his love in his own quiet way, but trust us—his heart is full of affection for those closest to him.

Dislikes

Aiko has his limits. He absolutely cannot stand being held for more than 5 seconds—any longer, and you risk unleashing his magical meow that could "explode" your eardrums. He also hates vacuum cleaners with a fiery passion and despises being left alone.

Aiko's Top Achievements

  1. Mastered the art of the 'tygydyk' at night.
  2. Successfully climbed into the smallest box in the house.
  3. Learned how to summon food with a single look.
  4. Became the loudest night singer in the neighborhood.
  5. Managed to avoid the vacuum cleaner for an entire year.

Superpowers

  • Summoning food at any time: With a single look (and perhaps a few meows), food magically appears!
  • Driving his mom and dad crazy with nighttime antics: Zoomies, singing, and midnight pranks are his specialty.
  • Magical meow: Hold him too long, and you might hear the "explosion"—a meow so powerful it could shake walls!
